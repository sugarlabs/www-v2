# About NSA Raiyyan
I'm a second-year undergrad at BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus. I started with C and C++, then moved into the JavaScript ecosystem during my second semester, working with React, Node.js, TypeScript, Redux, and Jest. Python came later through coursework and side projects, and I now use it for everything from scripting to working with ML libraries like PyTorch and HuggingFace Transformers.

Outside of code, I'm part of the [Department of Visual Media (DVM)](https://bits-dvm.org/) at BITS, where we build the official websites and apps for our major festivals, Oasis and APOGEE. These apps handle registrations, in-app wallets, and generate traffic of over 5000 users on our apps and websites, so I have hands-on experience shipping products that thousands of people use.
