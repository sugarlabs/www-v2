# About Stuti Jain

I am an undergraduate student in Chemical Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee and an open-source contributor passionate about building educational technology that makes learning interactive and accessible. My interests span full-stack development, AI, open-source software, and designing user-centered learning experiences.

This summer, I am contributing to **Music Blocks** through **DMP 2026** at Sugar Labs, where I am designing and implementing an educator-friendly lesson plan framework. My work focuses on integrating structured lesson plans directly into Music Blocks using story-driven learning, interactive challenges, and exploration-based activities to make music and programming concepts more engaging for young learners.

Alongside my open-source contributions, I lead development projects at the Information Management Group (IMG), IIT Roorkee, where I work on institute-scale web applications used by thousands of students.
